Description

This lovely lasagna makes everyone happy !

We are using the best ingredients

Ingredients

  • 500g powder
  • 500 ml milk
  • 100g butter cookies
  • 100g Cheese

Steps

  1. Take a bowl and insert 500ml milk into it
  2. Throw the 500g powder in and stir it
  3. Wait 5 minutes
  4. Throw the cheese and butter cookies in and stir again
  5. When the dough is creamy put it into a form
